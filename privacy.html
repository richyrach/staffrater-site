Privacy Policy

Who we are

Staff Rater is a Discord bot and lightweight web dashboard designed to help communities manage staff ratings, tickets, applications, leaderboards, and profiles. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Account basics: Discord user ID, username, and avatar when you sign in with Discord.
  • Server settings: selected channels/roles, feature toggles, forms, and preferences you configure.
  • Operational logs: basic request metadata (timestamps, endpoint, IP city/ASN) for security and abuse prevention.
  • Cookies/local storage: session tokens and consent preferences for a smoother experience.

Cookies & advertising

We use cookies/local storage for authentication and preferences. If you accept our banner, we also load Google AdSense/ads scripts which may use cookies or local storage to measure effectiveness and, where allowed, personalize ads. You can change your choice at any time by clearing site data or using your browser settings.

Third-party vendor: Google. Learn more at Google advertising policies and manage ad personalization at Ads Settings.

How we use data

  • Provide and improve bot features (ratings, tickets, applications, leaderboards, profiles).
  • Secure the service, detect abuse, and debug reliability issues.
  • Show and measure ads on the website when consent is granted.
  • Send essential service notices (e.g., breaking changes or outages).

Legal bases & your rights

Depending on your region, we process data under legitimate interests and/or your consent (for ads/analytics). Where applicable (e.g., EEA/UK), you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection to processing. For California users, you can request to know/delete certain information and opt out of certain sharing.

Data retention & security

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above, then delete or anonymize it. We use industry-standard safeguards; no method is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data.

Children

Staff Rater is not directed to children under 13 (or the age required by your region). Do not use the service if you are under the minimum age or if your server is primarily for children.

Contact

Questions or requests about this policy? Reach us via our Discord support server.

Changes

We may update this policy as we add features or comply with new requirements. We’ll update the “Last updated” date and, when appropriate, post a notice on the website.
